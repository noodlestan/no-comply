# Conventions WIP

## Conventions Consumer

**Use Cases:** Locating and reading convention sources and interpreting and applying their rules.

**Provides:**

- Processes
- Commands

**Capabilities:**

- Discover conventions that are relevant to the current context.
- Interpret conventions rules.
- Generate convention proposals.

## Conventions Producer

**Use Cases:** Growing and curating convention sources.

**Provides:**

- Processes
- Commands

**Capabilities:**

- Integrate convention proposals into references.
- Deduplicating and consolidating convention items.
