# Conventions Consumer API

**Use Cases:** Reading definitions of conventions files and drafts, and drafting convention proposals from friction.

**Provides:**

- Definitions
- Processes

**Capabilities:**

- Understand what conventions files and drafts are.
- Follow the process for writing convention drafts from friction.

## Mandatory Reading

::READ `definitions/index.md` - Essential definitions for conventions files and drafts.
